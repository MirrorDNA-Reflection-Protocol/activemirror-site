Core Principles

⟡ Reflective AI

AI that mirrors the user rather than predicts or manipulates them. The AI adapts to you — not the other way around. Your preferences, your voice, your context. Reflected, not overridden.

△ Trust by Design

Trust is structural, not promised. We don't ask you to trust us — we give you architecture that makes trust unnecessary. Local processing. Verifiable code. Auditable decisions.

◈ Sovereignty

You own and control your AI and identity. Not rented from a platform. Not held hostage by a vendor. Yours. Portable. Permanent. The way it should be.

⧉ Zero Drift

Maintaining truth and continuity over time. Every claim is classified as FACT, ESTIMATE, or UNKNOWN. No subtle distortions. No accumulated errors. Truth-state enforcement, not promises.

The Three Laws
